Chocoflan, also known as “pastel imposible” (impossible cake), is a delicious dessert that combines chocolate cake with creamy caramel flan in one magical dessert.

Arroz con Leche

Arroz con Leche, which translates to “rice with milk,” is a creamy and comforting rice pudding that is popular in many Latin American countries. It’s a delicious dessert that combines cooked rice with sweetened milk, cinnamon, and sometimes raisins.

Eat me!

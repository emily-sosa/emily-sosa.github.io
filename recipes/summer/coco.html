Summer

Agua de Coco

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of evaporated milk
  • 2 cups of coconut cream
  • 1 cup of Coconut Water
  • 1/2 cup of grated coconut
  • 4 cups of water

Instructions

Step 1: Mix

Add the evaporated milk to the blender, with the coconut cream, coconut water and water. Blend for 2 minutes.

Step 2: Additional flavor (optional)

For a more intense coconut flavor, incorporate 1/2 cup of grated coconut.

Step 3: Serve

Serve with ice and enjoy this hot season!

Arroz Con Leche

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1 cinnamon stick or 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup granulated sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • Ground cinnamon for garnish (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Rinse the Rice

Place the rice in a fine-mesh strainer and rinse it under cold running water until the water runs clear. This removes excess starch and helps prevent the rice pudding from becoming too thick.

Step 2: Boil the Rice

In a large, heavy-bottomed saucepan, combine the rinsed rice, 2 cups of milk, and the cinnamon stick (if using). Bring to a gentle boil over medium heat. Stir occasionally to prevent the rice from sticking to the bottom of the pot.

Step 3: Simmer

Reduce the heat to low and let the rice simmer, uncovered, until it absorbs most of the milk. Stir occasionally to ensure even cooking. This may take about 15-20 minutes.

Step 4: Add Remaining Ingredients

Add the remaining 2 cups of milk, sugar, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt to the pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

Step 5: Simmer Again

Allow the mixture to simmer over low heat, stirring frequently, for another 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the mixture thickens to your desired consistency.

Step 6: Remove the Cinnamon Stick

If you used a cinnamon stick, remove it from the mixture.

Step 7: Serve Warm or Cold

Arroz con leche can be served warm or cold, depending on your preference. You can let it cool at room temperature and then refrigerate it for a few hours if you prefer it cold.

Step 8: Garnish and Serve

Before serving, sprinkle ground cinnamon on top for garnish, if desired.

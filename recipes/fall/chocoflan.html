Chocoflan

Caramel:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar

Flan:

  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (12 ounces) evaporated milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Chocolate Flan:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk (or a mixture of 1/2 cup milk with 1/2 tablespoon white vinegar or lemon juice)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Place a large roasting pan or baking dish inside the oven and fill it with about 1 inch of hot water. This will create a water bath for the chocoflan.

Step 2: Prepare the Caramel

In a heavy saucepan over medium heat, melt 1 cup of sugar. Stir continuously until the sugar melts and turns into a golden caramel. Be careful not to burn it. Quickly pour the caramel into a 10-inch round bundt pan, swirling to coat the bottom evenly. Allow it to cool and harden.

Step 3: Prepare the Flan

In a blender, combine the sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, 3 eggs, and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract. Blend until smooth. Gently pour the flan mixture over the caramel layer in the bundt pan.

Step 4: Prepare the Chocolate Cake

In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, 1 cup of sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. In a separate bowl, mix the vegetable oil, buttermilk, 2 eggs, and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract. Combine the wet and dry ingredients, and mix until you have a smooth chocolate cake batter.

Step 5: Layer the Cake

Carefully pour the chocolate cake batter over the flan mixture in the bundt pan. The cake batter will float on top of the flan.

Step 6: Bake

Place the bundt pan with the chocoflan into the preheated oven on the rack above the water bath. Bake for about 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.

Step 7: Cool

Remove the chocoflan from the oven and allow it to cool in the pan for about 15-20 minutes.

Step 8: Invert and Serve

Carefully invert the chocoflan onto a serving plate. You may need to run a knife around the edge of the pan to help release it. The caramel will flow over the cake, creating a beautiful, glossy glaze.

Step 8: Chill

Once the chocoflan has cooled, refrigerate it for at least 3-4 hours or until well chilled. This allows the flavors to meld and makes it easier to slice.

Step 8: Slice and Serve

Slice and serve your chocoflan, enjoying the layers of flan and chocolate cake together.

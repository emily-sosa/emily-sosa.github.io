Winter

Galletas de Polvo

Cookie Dough:

  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Cinnamon-Sugar Coating:

  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone baking mats.

Step 2: Whisk Dry Ingredients

In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt. Set this dry mixture aside.

Step 3: Cream Butter and Sugar

In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and 1 1/2 cups of granulated sugar until the mixture becomes light and fluffy, which takes about 2-3 minutes.

Step 4: Add Eggs and Vanilla

Beat in the eggs one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract, mixing well after each addition

Step 5: Mix in the Dry Ingredients

Gradually add the dry ingredient mixture to the wet mixture, and mix until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix.

Step 6: Chill the Dough (Optional)

The dough can be a bit sticky. If it’s too soft to work with, you can cover the bowl and refrigerate it for about 30 minutes to an hour to make it easier to handle.

Step 7: Prepare Cinnamon-Sugar Coating

In a small bowl, mix together the 1/4 cup of granulated sugar and 2 teaspoons of ground cinnamon.

Step 8: Form Cookie Dough Balls

Scoop out portions of the cookie dough, about 1 to 1.5 tablespoons each, and roll them into balls. Roll each ball in the cinnamon-sugar coating to cover it completely.

Step 8: Place on Baking Sheets

Place the coated cookie dough balls on the prepared baking sheets, leaving some space between them to allow for spreading.

Step 8: Bake

Bake in the preheated oven for about 10-12 minutes or until the edges are slightly golden but the centers are still soft. Be careful not to overbake; the cookies will continue to set as they cool.

Step 8: Cool and Serve

Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

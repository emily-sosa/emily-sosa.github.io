Hummingbird Cake

For the Cake

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of mashed ripe bananas (about 3 bananas)
  • 1 cup crushed pineapple, drained
  • 1 cup chopped pecans

For Cream Cheese Frosting

  • 8 oz (1 package) cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour three 9-inch round cake pans

Step 2: Mix Dry Ingredients

In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg.

Step 3: Combine Wet Ingredients

In another bowl, beat the eggs, and then add the vegetable oil and vanilla extract. Mix well.

Step 4: Mix the batter

Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined. Don’t over-mix.

Step 5: Fold in Fruit and Nuts

Gently fold in the mashed bananas, crushed pineapple, and chopped pecans until they are evenly distributed throughout the batter.

Step 6: Divide and Bake

Divide the batter equally among the prepared cake pans. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Step 7: Cool

Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for about 10 minutes, then remove them from the pans and place them on a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 8: Make the Cream Cheese Frosting

In a separate bowl, beat the cream cheese and butter together until smooth and creamy. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract, and beat until well combined.

Step 9: Assemble the Cake

Once the cakes are completely cooled, spread a layer of cream cheese frosting on top of one cake layer. Place the second cake layer on top and repeat. Finally, add the third cake layer, and frost the top and sides of the entire cake.

Step 10: Decorate

If desired, you can decorate the cake with additional pecans, shredded coconut, or banana slices.

Carrot Cake

For the Cake

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 cups grated carrots (about 3-4 medium carrots)
  • 1/2 cup crushed pineapple, drained
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans (optional)
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger

For Cream Cheese Frosting

  • 8 oz (1 package) cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans or one 9x13-inch baking pan.

Step 2: Mix Dry Ingredients

In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and ginger. Mix them together until well combined.

Step 3: Mix Wet Ingredients

In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and then add the vegetable oil. Mix well.

Step 4: Mix Batter

Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until the batter is smooth.

Step 5: Add Carrots and ` Fruits

Stir in the grated carrots, crushed pineapple, and chopped nuts or shredded coconut (if using). Mix until these ingredients are evenly distributed in the batter.

Step 6: Pour into Pan

Pour the batter into the prepared cake pans.

Step 7: Bake

Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes for the round pans or 30-35 minutes for the 9x13-inch pan. The cake is done when a toothpick or cake tester inserted into the center comes out clean.

Step 8: Cool

Remove the cakes from the oven and let them cool in the pans for about 10 minutes. Then, remove the cakes from the pans and place them on a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 9: Prepare Frosting

While the cakes are cooling, prepare the cream cheese frosting. In a mixing bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and butter until smooth. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla extract, and continue to beat until the frosting is creamy and well combined.

Step 10: Decorate with Frosting

Once the cakes are completely cool, spread a layer of cream cheese frosting on top of one of the cakes. Place the second cake on top and frost the top and sides of the entire cake.

Step 11: Additional Decorative elements

You can decorate your carrot cake with additional chopped nuts or a sprinkle of shredded coconut if you like.

Step 12: Enjoy!

Slice and enjoy your homemade carrot cake!
